Three-Level Web Map Structure

The largest categories are NO2, PM2.5, and PM10. Each pollutant section contains three thematic map views: pollution, land cover, and population.

NO2 Nitrogen dioxide Pollution, land cover, and population maps. PM2.5 Fine particulate matter Pollution, land cover, and population maps. PM10 Coarse particulate matter Pollution, land cover, and population maps.
