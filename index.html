GeoServer Connection Notes

The map viewer is prepared for layers published from TIF or GPKG data through GeoServer WMS. Fill in data-geoserver-url and the three data-layer-* values in the map container when the final layers are ready.

Layer option Reserved GeoServer layer name Status
Pollution your_workspace:pollution Waiting for GeoServer WMS
Landcover your_workspace:landcover Waiting for GeoServer WMS
Population your_workspace:population Waiting for GeoServer WMS
